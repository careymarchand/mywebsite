What is a Millennial?

Anyone born between 1981 and 1996 (ages 22-37 in 2018) will be considered a Millennial, and anyone born from 1997 onward is to be part of a new generation.

Anyone born after 1996 is part of a new, yet-to-be named generation that is being reffered to as “post Millennials” until a better name is established.
